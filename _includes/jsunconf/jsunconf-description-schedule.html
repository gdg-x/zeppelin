JSUnconf is an Unconference.
That means the schedule is voted by the participants.

Our plan is to offer an initial session on Saturday, March 25th 2017.
In this session we want to give you the most important information to get through our tutorial.
Afterwards we will offer you the help of our volunteer mentors during office hours, where you can get help with the tutorial.

You are free to go through the tutorial at our office hours place or during the sessions and breaks of the conference.

  • 12:30 - Workshop begins!
  • 13:15 - Lunch break

  • 15:45 - Coffee Break
  • 16:30 - Office Hours
